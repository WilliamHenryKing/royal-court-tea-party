# The Royal Court Tea

## Technologies of the Realm
- Three.js (CDN) for the 3D village, characters, and camera effects.
- Web Audio for the royal playlist and in-world sound cues.

## Crowned Purpose
The Royal Court Tea is a playful, story-led web experience that doubles as an invitation to a themed tea gathering. Guests wander a whimsical royal village, collect sweet treats, and meet the court to learn the event details, guest list, and feast expectations.

## What Awaits You
- Explore a charming village and follow the royal paths between five key locations.
- Speak with the court to uncover event details, the speaker lineup, and the guest registry.
- Collect sweets to gain a brief burst of speed.
- Enjoy a curated music playlist while you roam.
- Track progress as you visit each landmark in the kingdom.

## How to Play
1. Enter the kingdom from the splash screen.
2. Move with the on-screen joystick or enable desktop controls.
3. Approach a character and interact to hear their story or guidance.
4. Visit all five primary locations to complete the royal quest.
